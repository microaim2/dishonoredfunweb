Key Figures

Faces of Conspiracy

Assassins, nobles, and visionaries pulling the city toward ruin or redemption.

Corvo Attano

Corvo Attano is the masked protagonist of Dishonored — a royal bodyguard framed for the Empress’s murder who becomes a supernatural assassin seeking revenge.

Emily Kaldwin

Emily Kaldwin is the daughter of the Empress in Dishonored. After her mother is killed, she is kidnapped and later rescued by Corvo.

Jessamine Kaldwin

Jessamine Kaldwin is the Empress of Dunwall in Dishonored. She is assassinated at the beginning of the game, which leads to Corvo being framed and the main events of the story.

The Outsider

The Outsider is a mysterious supernatural being in Dishonored who grants special powers to certain people, including Corvo.

Daud

Daud is the leader of the assassins called the Whalers in Dishonored and the man who kills Empress Jessamine.

Farley Havelock

Farley Havelock is a former admiral and leader of the Loyalists in Dishonored who later betrays Corvo to gain power.

Ramsey

Mortimer Ramsey is the captain of the Grand Guard in Dishonored 2 who betrays Emily and helps start the coup.

Hiram Burrows

Hiram Burrows is the Lord Regent in Dishonored who helps plan the assassination of the Empress and rules Dunwall afterward.

Thaddeus Campbell

Thaddeus Campbell is the High Overseer of the Abbey of the Everyman in Dishonored and one of the conspirators against the Empress.

Custis Pandleton, Morgan Pandleton

Wealthy twin brothers in Dishonored who help organize the plot against the Empress.

Anton Sokolov

Anton Sokolov is a famous inventor and natural philosopher in Dishonored who creates many technologies used in Dunwall.

Samuel Beechworth

Samuel Beechworth is a loyal boatman in Dishonored who helps Corvo travel to missions around Dunwall.

Slackjaw

Slackjaw is the leader of the Bottle Street Gang in Dishonored who sometimes helps Corvo.

Billy Lurk

Billie Lurk is an assassin and member of Daud’s Whalers in Dishonored who later becomes an important character in the series.

Granny Rags

Granny Rags is a mysterious old woman with supernatural powers in Dishonored who worships the Outsider.

Lydia

Lydia is a maid at the Hound Pits Pub in Dishonored who helps the Loyalists.

Cecilia

Cecilia is a servant at the Hound Pits Pub in Dishonored who supports the Loyalists.

Wallace Higgins

Wallace Higgins is a civilian in Dishonored whom Corvo can help during a side mission.

Callista Curnow

Callista Curnow is Emily’s caretaker in Dishonored and helps the Loyalists at the Hound Pits Pub.

Griff

Griff is a merchant and smuggler in Dishonored who sells supplies to Corvo.

Geoff Curnow

Geoff Curnow is a loyal City Watch captain in Dishonored who can be saved from an assassination plot.

Overseer Berthold

Overseer Berthold is a member of the Abbey of the Everyman in Dishonored who helps Corvo during the mission against High Overseer Campbell.

Trevor Pandleton

Trevor Pendleton is a member of the Loyalists in Dishonored who helps Corvo overthrow the Lord Regent.

Madame Prudences

Madame Prudence is the owner of the Golden Cat in Dishonored where Emily is held captive.

Lady Boyle

Lady Boyle is a noblewoman in Dishonored who secretly supports the Lord Regent.

Lord Montgomerry Shaw

Lord Montgomery Shaw is a nobleman in Dishonored whom Corvo can duel at the Boyle masquerade party.

Lord Brisby

Lord Brisby is a nobleman in Dishonored who is obsessed with Lady Boyle and offers to take her away.

Piero Joplin

Piero Joplin is an inventor in Dishonored who creates gadgets and upgrades for Corvo.

Blake

Blake is a servant at the Hound Pits Pub in Dishonored who works for the Loyalists.

Teague Martin

Teague Martin is an Overseer and member of the Loyalists in Dishonored who helps plan the overthrow of the Lord Regent.

Bundry Rothwild

Bundry Rothwild is a wealthy whaling baron in Dishonored DLC Knife of Dunwall and an enemy of Daud.

Arnold Timsh

Arnold Timsh is a corrupt lawyer in the DLC of Dishonored who works with the Brigmore Witches.
